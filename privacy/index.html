Privacy

Built around official access and careful handling.

Stats is designed to give Tesla owners deep insight and powerful controls while staying careful about authentication, data handling, and unnecessary vehicle wakeups.

How access works

Stats uses Tesla-hosted authentication flows and official Tesla API support, which means your Tesla email address and password are not entered into Stats itself.

The app is designed to surface rich information and controls while being mindful about unnecessary vehicle wakeups, helping reduce avoidable phantom-drain impact.

Stats does not share your data with any other company, service, or third party.

Need help?

If you have a privacy-related question, the fastest path is still the Support button inside the Stats app. You can also reach out directly by email.
