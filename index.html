Stats for Tesla

Enhance your Tesla experience with detailed analytics and control features.

Key Features

  • Phantom Drain Monitoring: Operates without waking your car.
  • Live Charging Activity: Real-time charging status on lock screen widgets.
  • Lock Screen Widgets: Battery and temperature details without waking the car.
  • Smart Window Venting: Automatic or manual window control based on temperature.
  • Sentry View: View Sentry Mode videos and combine them with timestamps.
  • Notifications: Alerts for open doors or active climate control.
  • Cost Savings Analysis: Compare costs with a traditional gasoline vehicle.
  • Siri Integration: Control your car using Siri Shortcuts.
  • Performance Comparisons: Compare efficiency metrics with other users.
  • Charging Insights: Analyze charge rates during sessions.
  • Battery Health Tracking: Monitor maximum range over time.
  • Charge Reminders: Notifications for low battery at specific locations.
  • Climate Scheduling: Set climate control for specific times.
  • Battery Pre-Conditioning: Automatic top-off charge before usage.
